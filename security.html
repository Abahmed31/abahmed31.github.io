Data Safety & Compliance

Security-first infrastructure. Zero storage of sensitive data. Full transparency.

No PII Policy

For legal compliance and ethical responsibility, we do not accept personally identifiable information (PII), protected health information (PHI), or any sensitive personal records.

  • No names, SSNs, addresses, DOBs
  • No medical or clinical data
  • No personnel files or HR-sensitive items
  • No disciplinary documentation

Security Measures

Encryption

All data transferred is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

Minimal Data Retention

We only keep non-sensitive operational datasets required to generate your reports.

Segregated Processing

Workspaces are segregated to prevent cross-organization mixing.

Access Control

Only authorized analysts can access your uploads.

Audit Trails

Processing actions are logged and monitored.

Secure Deletion

Your data is removed after report delivery or by request.
