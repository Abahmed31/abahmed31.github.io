Our Solutions — detailed

Overtime Optimization

We locate overtime drivers by analyzing historical rosters, payroll, and policy rules. Our method isolates repeated causes (e.g., shift rounding, minimum-call rules, understaffed windows) and prescribes targeted operational changes.

Included activities

  • Root-cause analysis of overtime events
  • Rule-based detection (policy + scheduling engine checks)
  • Prioritized action list: schedule rules, capacity changes, and role adjustments

Deliverables

  • One-page prioritized action plan
  • Estimated monthly OT savings with confidence ranges
  • Implementation difficulty & owner assignment

Burnout & Risk Detection

We evaluate shift patterns, consecutive work windows, and high-intensity assignments to surface teams and individuals at elevated risk for fatigue-related issues.

Core analytics

  • Consecutive shifts and short-rest interval analysis
  • Distribution of weekend/holiday duty and on-call load
  • Floating/double-up frequency and clustering

Outcomes

  • Risk heatmaps and prioritized mitigation actions
  • Estimated impact on turnover/absenteeism (directional)
  • Policy suggestions to restore recovery windows

Predictive Forecasting (30 / 90 / 180 days)

We provide three forecast horizons: short-term (30 days), tactical (90 days), and strategic (180 days). Models combine historical seasonal patterns, local events, and trend analysis.

Forecast types & purpose

  • 30-day: minimize reactive OT and agency use — supports scheduling decisions.
  • 90-day: hiring & training planning, predictable peak windows, and leave management.
  • 180-day: budgeting, headcount planning, and strategic workforce initiatives.

Forecast outputs

  • Daily/weekly demand curves with confidence bands
  • Suggested staffing targets & recommended buffers
  • Scenario simulations showing projected OT & agency costs

Cost-Efficiency Modeling

We quantify the financial impact of scheduling decisions and proposed fixes — including OT, agency, and vacancy costs — and compare the ROI of options (hire vs. reschedule vs. use of flexible pools).

What's included

  • Detailed cost model for labor categories
  • Scenario comparison with ROI calculation
  • Break-even timelines for staffing changes

Dashboards & Integrations

Custom dashboards for executives, operations, and schedulers — with automated reports on your cadence. Data ingestion supports CSV and common payroll/scheduling APIs where available.

Capabilities

  • Executive-level KPIs & drill-down reports
  • Scheduler-facing daily sheets and exception lists
  • Automated email or dashboard deliveries (daily/weekly/monthly)

Implementation & Training

We help operationalize recommendations — change management, scheduler training, and monitoring to ensure sustained improvements.

Support options

  • Implementation playbook and roll-out plan
  • Scheduler training sessions (remote or on-site)
  • Ongoing monitoring and quarterly check-ins
